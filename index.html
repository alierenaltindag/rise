Rise Bot

Rise is a powerful Discord moderation bot designed to help server administrators maintain a safe and friendly environment. With customizable settings and easy-to-use commands, Rise streamlines your moderation tasks.

Privacy Policy

At Rise, we take your privacy seriously. Here's what you need to know about our data practices:
